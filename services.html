We Offer a Range of Services to Support Your Health

Our hospital provides essential medical services tailored to the daily needs of families in Timergara and surrounding areas. From common illnesses to maternal care, you’ll find professional help here.

General OPD

Consultations for common illnesses, follow-ups, and health concerns — handled by experienced physicians.

Emergency Services

24/7 emergency care for accidents, injuries, high fever, or serious conditions needing urgent attention.

Mother & Child Care

Antenatal care, safe deliveries, postnatal checkups, and child vaccinations under trusted supervision.

Lab Tests

Affordable lab testing for blood, sugar, urine, and more — with same-day or next-day results.

Women’s Health

Respectful care from female doctors for pregnancy, gynecology, family planning, and counseling.

X-Ray & Ultrasound

Basic imaging services for diagnostics, pregnancy scans, and injuries — all on-site.

Chronic Condition Care

Management of diabetes, blood pressure, and long-term illness with regular checkups and support.

Vaccinations

Routine childhood vaccines, seasonal flu shots, and COVID boosters as per government guidelines.
