About TCHKPK Hospital

Modern healthcare, deeply rooted in local values — serving the people of Timergara and beyond.

Who We Are

TCHKPK is a newly established general hospital in the heart of Timergara, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa. We were created to bring accessible, affordable, and respectful healthcare to our local community — where care is often hard to reach and even harder to afford.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted healthcare provider in Timergara, known for patient respect, community care, and affordable access to essential medical services.

Our Mission

We aim to deliver clean, honest, and effective care — whether it’s a child's fever, a safe delivery, or a sugar check for your elder. Our mission is grounded in dignity and compassion, not just treatment.

Why We Exist

In rural and underserved regions like Lower Dir and Timergara, families often travel far to find medical help. TCHKPK was built to change that — to offer reliable care locally, with staff that speaks your language and respects your culture.

Who We Serve

  • ✔ Families in Timergara and surrounding towns
  • ✔ Children in need of vaccinations & checkups
  • ✔ Women seeking safe and private maternity care
  • ✔ Emergency cases needing fast, first-level help
  • ✔ Elders managing diabetes, BP, and chronic conditions
